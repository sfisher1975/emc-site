What We Do

Troubleshooting & Repair

Fast, reliable diagnostics when your machines fail — no guesswork, just solutions. We handle electrical faults, sensor errors, control failures, and HMI issues with precision. Experienced with Allen-Bradley, Siemens, Red Lion, and other leading platforms.

HMI & PLC Services

We restore and optimize your machine control systems. From touchscreen HMIs to PLC logic, we offer reprogramming, troubleshooting, backups, and complete screen recovery. Whether it’s a faulted program or a corrupted display, we’ll get you back online fast.

Control Panel Modernization

Outdated panel? We’ll upgrade your system with modern, code-compliant hardware built for safety, serviceability, and performance. Clean layouts, proper labeling, and full documentation included with every job.

Preventive Maintenance

Reduce unplanned downtime with regular inspections, PLC backups, battery checks, and cleanup of control cabinets. We identify problems before they stop production — keeping your shop running smoothly.

Dashboards & Remote Monitoring

See your equipment data in real time — anytime, anywhere. We build custom dashboards with live metrics, alert notifications, and remote access from your phone or PC. Ideal for owners, supervisors, and maintenance leads.
